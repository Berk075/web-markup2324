About us

VisitZaanstad

Zaanstad is a beautiful city located between Amsterdam and Haarlem. The city has recently experienced growth in terms of tourists visiting the city. But a real guide was missing. With this website we want to fill that gap.

Our Goal

Our goal is to promote tourism in Zaanstad through this website. An increase has already been visible in recent years. This is partly because tourists who come to the big cities find hotel prices too high compared to Zaanstad. In addition, it is also very easy to get from the center of Zaanstad to the big cities by public transport. But this trend is not accompanied by visiting the beautiful places in the city, and we want to change that.
